# Outreach Kit — Validation Sprint (agencies)

_Created 2026-06-24. The copy-paste execution sheet for the 2-week validation sprint. Pairs with
`go-to-market-runbook.md` (strategy) and `templates/evidence-record-template.html` (the deliverable)._

## The goal (don't lose sight of it)
Get **2–3 agencies to say "yes, I'd pay $X to white-label this"** (or sign an LOI / pre-pay). That's the gate.
You are NOT trying to close deals or get sign-ups — you're trying to find out if the evidence record is worth paying for. ~50 contacted → ~10 conversations → 2–4 will-pay. **Kill at <2 after 2 weeks.**

---

## Step 1 — Who qualifies (ICP filters)
A prospect goes on the list only if it's:
- **US-based** (the live lane — US ADA litigation is the real pain; EU/EAA is taken by a €39/yr incumbent).
- **A small web/design/dev agency, ~2–20 people** (big enough to have a book of clients; small enough that the founder reads their own DMs).
- **Builds/maintains sites for SMBs — especially e-commerce / Shopify / WordPress** (e-comm is ~70% of ADA web suits, so *their clients are the targets* → accessibility is a real, sellable risk for them).
- **Has a public portfolio** (you need a real client site to scan — that's the hook).
- **Founder/owner/principal reachable** on LinkedIn or via a real email (not a generic info@ only).

Bonus signal (prioritize these): agencies that **already sell audits/maintenance retainers** (they have a deliverable to slot this into) or **post about ADA/accessibility** (warm).

Skip: solo freelancers (no client book), big 50+ agencies (no founder access), pure-overlay resellers, EU/EAA-only shops.

---

## Step 2 — Where to pull them (build 60–80 in ~30 min)
Work warmest → coldest:

1. **Your own network first.** Anyone you know who runs/works at a web agency. People-you-know convert first — start here.
2. **Shopify Partners directory** → `shopify.com/partners/directory` — filter to US, "Website design and development." *Their clients are Shopify stores = the exact ADA-lawsuit targets.* Best single source.
3. **Clutch** → `clutch.co/web-designers` → filters: Location = **United States**, Company size = **2–9** and **10–49**, Service = **Web Design / E-Commerce Development**. Profiles list real clients → grab a client URL to scan.
4. **Directory sweep:** DesignRush, UpCity, GoodFirms (same filters).
5. **LinkedIn search strings** (paste, then filter to founders/owners):
   - `"web design agency" (founder OR owner OR principal) United States`
   - `"we build Shopify stores"` · `"Shopify Plus partner" agency`
   - `"WordPress agency" (founder OR owner)`
6. **Google strings:**
   - `site:clutch.co web designers "United States" shopify`
   - `"web design agency" "our work" OR "portfolio" shopify -jobs`

Log each into the tracker (Step 5) with one **client site you'll scan** as the personalization hook.

---

## Step 3 — Per-prospect workflow (the hook)
For each agency, before you message:
1. Pick **one client site** from their portfolio.
2. Run **A11y Beast** on it (the live scanner).
3. Fill `templates/evidence-record-template.html` with the real output → **Print → Save as PDF**. Swap the logo slot + "[Your Agency Name]" → *their* agency, so they see it white-labeled as theirs.
4. Now you have a personalized gift to lead with. Send.

This is the whole edge: you're not pitching a tool, you're handing them a finished, branded deliverable for *their* client.

---

## Step 4 — The messages (copy, personalize the [brackets])
Tone rule: **lead with the gift, frame it as "I'm learning," zero fear-mongering, zero "compliant/lawsuit-proof" claims.** Help, never threat.

**4a — LinkedIn connect note (≤300 chars):**
> Hi [Name] — I built a tool that turns an accessibility scan into a dated, defensible *evidence record*. I ran it on [client site] you built and made you the 1-pager (your brand on it). Happy to send it over — genuinely no pitch, just learning if this is useful to agencies.

**4b — Cold email:**
> **Subject:** made you an accessibility 1-pager for [Client]
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I ran an accessibility scan on [client site] — one of the sites in your portfolio — and turned it into a dated, defensible **evidence record**: each issue mapped to the laws it implicates (ADA, Unruh, EAA…), plus the fixes, as a one-page PDF you could hand a client.
>
> I build these to be **white-labeled by agencies** — your logo, your deliverable. Want the [Client] report I already made? Free, no strings — I'm mostly trying to learn whether this is actually useful to agencies like yours.
>
> [Your name]

**4c — Follow-up #1 (+3 days, attach the PDF):**
> Hi [Name] — here's the [Client] evidence record I mentioned (attached). The useful part for clients is the dated, tamper-evident bit: documented proof they assessed accessibility and what they're fixing, if it's ever questioned. Is a white-labeled version of this something you'd use in your audits or retainers?

**4d — Follow-up #2 (+4 days, last — friendly):**
> Last note, [Name] — should I keep you in the loop as this develops, or is it just not a fit? Either way the [Client] report is yours to use. Thanks for the read.

**4e — The will-pay ask (once they reply / on a 15-min call):**
> "If this were white-labeled under your brand — your logo, client-ready evidence records, re-runnable to show a client's progress over time — would you put it in your audit or retainer packages? And ballpark: what would you charge a client for it, or pay per client / per month?"
>
> → Then **stop talking and listen.** Write down the exact number and words. That's the research.

## Step 6 — Cadence (2 weeks)
- **Days 1–2:** build the 60–80 list (Step 2).
- **Days 2–10:** ~**10–15 prospects/day** — for each: scan a client site, make the PDF, send Touch 1 (LinkedIn *or* email). Send Touch 2 / Touch 3 on the +3 / +4 day schedule. Book 15-min calls with anyone interested.
- **Days 5–14:** the conversations + the will-pay ask. Log everything.
- **Day 14:** read the tracker → the gate.

---

## Step 7 — The gate (decide BEFORE you start, so you don't move it)
- **≥2–3 "yes, I'd pay $X" / LOI / pre-pay → VALIDATED.** Now reprice around what they said, build the durable evidence document + accounts + monitoring (in that order), settle the name, fix the AI-look, *then* launch.
- **0–1 → STOP or pivot the wedge** (e.g., try the just-sued-SMB cohort, or rethink the offer). The product's built — nothing more is owed to it. Do not "just keep building."

---

## Notes
- One conversation with an agency that says yes is worth more than 100 sign-ups — agencies resell, so each is many client sites.
- If email deliverability is shaky, lead on LinkedIn DMs (founders read them).
- Never claim "compliant," "certified," or "lawsuit-proof." The evidence record's honesty notice is the differentiator — let it do the work.
